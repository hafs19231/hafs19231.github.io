## Prompts

There are 9 types of prompts with different colors.

> **warning prompt**: warning
{: .prompt-warning}

> **announce prompt**: announcement, news, notice
{: .prompt-announce}

> **info prompt**: definition, explanation
{: .prompt-info}

> **tip prompt**: tips, tricks, and notes
{: .prompt-tip}

> **checkbox prompt**: example, exercise
{: .prompt-checkbox}

> **theorem prompt**: mathematical theorem and lemma, scientific theorem
{: .prompt-theorem}

> **problem prompt**: math&science problems, programming problems
{: .prompt-problem }

> **quote prompt**: quote, citation
{: .prompt-quote}

> **code prompt**: code, programming, terminal
{: .prompt-code}
